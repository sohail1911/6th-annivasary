6 Years of Us

From classroom dreams to our forever home.

January 1, 2020

The Proposal

I spent that entire day at your home—a day I'll never forget. As I was leaving, I asked you that one question that changed our lives forever:

"Naama en kalyanam pannika kudatu?"

We were just in the last 3 months of 12th standard, but even then, I knew.

2020 - 2023

The Distance & The Strength

Then the pandemic hit. We couldn't meet, and the mental pressure at home was heavy. Talking to you was my only relief—you saved me. Even through different colleges and the rare sightings, we fought to stay close.

June 21, 2024

The Sleeper Bus Memory

That beautiful day in Chennai and that unforgettable night on the sleeper bus. Just you and me, heading home, making memories that would last a lifetime.

August 10, 2025

Our First Date

The reward for all those years of waiting. Finally, a date that belonged only to us.

2025 was a year of storms. I know I made you cry and I know I hurt you. It breaks my heart because you have been my everything. My promise to you starts on 1/1/2026: I will be calm. I will be your peace. I will never hurt you again.

You have been my best friend, my lover, and my soulmate. Soon, you are going to be my pondati. You are my Ilakk. You aren't just a person to me; you are my home and I am yours too. Working beside you now is a dream come true.

Lets get married soon, do all the things we shared and start to live together.
